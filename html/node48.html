Reference orbit display

This operation computes the orbit defined by the initial coordinates x x' y y' , and provides either a printout or a printer-plot display. 

Input format 


REFErence ........(maximum 80 characters) 


nprint sizex sizey 

 


npos  for i from 1 to npos.
Parameter definitions

nprint controls display

1 a printout is provided

2 a printer-plot is provided

11,12

21,22

sizex,sizey
.
. npos
